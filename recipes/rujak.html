Rujak Buah (spicy sweet fruit salad)

Description

Indonesian spicy fruit salad is yummy

Ingredients

  • 50 gram fried peanut
  • 50 gram palm sugar
  • 1 bit tamarind
  • 5 red chili
  • 1 bit salt
  • 4 tbsp water
  • 1 pieces fried garlic
  • 1 bit shrimp paste
  • Fruits (any fruit you have, you can add fried tofu if you like)

Directions

  • Mash the fried peanut, chili, salt, garlic, tamarind, shrimp paste, and palm sugar until smooth. Add water, mix with spoon
  • Cut any fruit you have
  • You can dip your slice fruits into hot salad, enjoy it ðŸ˜Š
